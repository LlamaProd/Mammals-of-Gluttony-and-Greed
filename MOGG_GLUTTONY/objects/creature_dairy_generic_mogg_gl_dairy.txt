creature_dairy_generic_mogg_gl_dairy

These are purely for use in referencing dairy in other creatures, to keep the various dairy products in more concise categories rather than defining each on on a per-creature level. !!
			!! You can see examples of how these can be used in c_variation_dairy and the creature raws that are referencing those variations !!
